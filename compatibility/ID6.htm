About

Game Title:
Initial D: Arcade Stage 6 Double Ace

Hardware:
SEGA RingEdge

Game Info:
A new mode, Tag Battle, was introduced. Unlike its predecessor, it was released for the Sega RingEdge hardware for the first time. The game saw a change in its driving physics. Unlike Initial D Arcade Stage 5, a drift gauge and an updated tachometer were introduced to help inform players when they were drifting. The 'Legend of the Street' mode also introduced a "Rolling Start" since the story mode in Initial D Special Stage. The tachometer now informed players whether or not their speed was below the gear range. The game also added a mileage system to show players their total driven mileage. Mileages could be used to redeem exclusive cars that were later available in updates.

Setup

Game

  • Executable is "id6_dump_.exe" found in game's root folder.

AMD Fix

  • Download fix here.
  • Open fix archive and open "D6-7-8" folder.
  • Extract contents into game's root folder, replacing existing files.
  • Enable "General - EnableAmdFix" in GAME SETTINGS.

Common Issues / Questions

Car pulls left or right

Make sure your controller was completely centered during setup for first time. If it's still pulling to one side, enter the TEST menu and calibrate the center point of the wheel axis.

Stuck on error in red box during boot

Press the View Change button to continue past the error.
